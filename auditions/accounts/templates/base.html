SAEINDIA is one of the few professional engineering societies whose membership
represents practically every engineering and scientific discipline.

Its members combine their specialized abilities to further advance the research, development, design, manufacture and utilization of vehicles which operate on land and water and in air and space.

Members receive free registration at SAEINDIA's lecture meetings and exhibits.
